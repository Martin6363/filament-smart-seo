### Added

- Polymorphic `seo_metadata` storage with `HasSeo` trait
- `SeoSection` Filament form component with fluent source-field mapping
- Live Google SERP preview (pure Tailwind / Blade, no NPM packages)
- Gemini-powered title, description, and keyword generation
- Spatie Translatable and locale-suffixed multi-language layouts
- Settings store + `InteractsWithSeoSettings` for custom Filament pages
- Publishable config, migrations, translations, and views
